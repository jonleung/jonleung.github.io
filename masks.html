Do I even need an n95 mask?

So from my research, because n95 masks are very uncomfortable and hard to wear properly, they only recommend wearing an n95 mask when you're in higher risk situations and defaulting to a surgical mask otherwise.

Wearing n95 masks can backfire if:

  • If you wear it improperly (see the next section)
  • because they are uncomfortable, you keep touching it and  touch your face
  • you touch the front part of the mask after it's being worn contaminating your hands
  • If you save the n95 mask and put it in your purse it will contaminate your purse
  • you put it on without washing your hands which will contaminate your face
  • you keep wearing the same mask past 8 hours and the mask and the moisture of you breathing breeds germs
