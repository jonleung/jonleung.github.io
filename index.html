Jonathan Leung

Jonathan is a product focused full stack engineer. He is passionate about building experiences that spark joy and has an eye on simplifying what's built to iterate towards business goals quickly.

Previously, Jonathan was the sole web engineer at Calm when they won Apple's App of the Year. Before that, Jonathan was recognized as Forbes 30 Under 30 in education for co-founding the international non-profit tech education startup, Hack Club. Jonathan was the lead organizer of PennApps, the first and largest intercollegiate hackathon in the world, when he studied computer engineering at the University of Pennsylvania.
